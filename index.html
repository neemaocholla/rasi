Rasi's Array of Screen Printing Techniques

Dive into the world of possibilities with Rasi's diverse range of screen printing
techniques.From traditional methods to cutting-edge innovations.

Traditional Textile
Screen Printing

This method is extensively employed in the fashion
and apparel industry for printing designs onto
garments,fabrics,and accessories.

Advanced UV
Screen Printing

UV screen printing has gained popularity due to its ability to cure instantly when exposed to ultraviolet light,resulting in fast production.

Four-Color
Process Printing

Also known as CYMK printing,this method is widely
used for reproducing full-color images with a broad
range of tones and gradients.
